Ngozi Okonjo-Iweala is a Nigerian-born economist and international development expert. She sits on the Boards of Standard Chartered Bank , Twitter, Global Alliance for Vaccines and Immunization (GAVI) , and the African Risk Capacity (ARC) . Previously, Okonjo-Iweala spent a 25-year career at the World Bank as a development economist, scaling the ranks to the Number 2 position of Managing Director, Operations.
